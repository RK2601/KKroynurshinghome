About Us

Dedicated care for the people of Muzaffarpur

KK Roy Nurshing Home is a family-run healthcare facility committed to safe, ethical, and compassionate care. Our aim is to bring modern medical services to Bihar while keeping the warmth of a community clinic.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality treatment with empathy and dignity for every patient who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted nursing home in North Bihar through excellent clinical outcomes and patient-first service.

Values We Live By

Integrity, safety, transparency, and respect guide every decision we make at KK Roy Nurshing Home.
